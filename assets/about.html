Our Commitment to Your Well-being

The LIU-Brooklyn Psychological Services Center is dedicated to providing free, confidential counseling to all eligible LIU-Brooklyn students. We understand that navigating college life can present a variety of challenges, and we're here to help you through them.

Our commitment extends to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and respected. We offer a space of safety, free from judgment. We have no session limits or requirements to disclose your identity. You remain eligible for services as long as you are a student at LIU.
