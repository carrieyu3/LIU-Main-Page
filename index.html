Your Path to Mental Well-being

Providing confidential and supportive counseling for LIU-Brooklyn students.

Types of Support You Can Find

We offer confidential counseling for a range of student needs, supporting your emotional and academic well-being.

Navigating Academic Pressures

College life can be demanding. We offer support for managing academic stress, test anxiety, and time management challenges. Let us help you achieve your academic goals while maintaining your well-being.

Finding Balance at Home

Family dynamics and home life can sometimes be sources of stress. Our counselors provide a safe space to explore and address these challenges, helping you build healthier relationships and coping strategies.

Building Confidence in Social Settings

Navigating social situations can feel overwhelming. We provide guidance to help you develop strong communication skills, manage social anxiety, and build meaningful connections. Gain the confidence to engage in conversations and create lasting relationships.

Our Commitment to Your Well-Being

The LIU-Brooklyn Psychological Services Center is dedicated to providing free, confidential counseling to all enrolled LIU-Brooklyn students. We understand that navigating college life can present a variety of challenges, and we're here to help you through them. Our commitment extends to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and respected. We offer a space of safety, trust, and openness, regardless of your background or identity. We have no session limits, so we are here for you for as long as you are a student at LIU.

Our Commitment to Your Well-being

The LIU-Brooklyn Psychological Services Center is dedicated to providing free, confidential counseling to all eligible LIU-Brooklyn students. We understand that navigating college life can present a variety of challenges, and we're here to help you through them.

Our commitment extends to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and respected. We offer a space of safety, free from judgment. We have no session limits or requirements to disclose your identity. You remain eligible for services as long as you are a student at LIU.

Frequently Asked Questions

Confidentiality is a priority. Your privacy is protected by strict confidentiality policies. Your information will not be shared without your explicit consent, except in cases of immediate danger.

You can make an appointment with LIU counselors through the 'Book Appointment' button. From there, you will be able to select a counselor of your choosing based on the time and date you are available.
